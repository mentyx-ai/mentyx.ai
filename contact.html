Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Typical implementation takes 4-8 weeks, including data migration, team training, and workflow customization. We provide dedicated support throughout the entire process.

Do you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. Mentyx integrates with major CRM platforms, accounting systems, and data providers via API. We'll work with your team to ensure seamless data flow.

What security certifications do you have?

Mentyx is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. We use bank-grade AES-256 encryption, maintain full audit trails, and undergo regular penetration testing.

Can we customize workflows and underwriting rules?

Absolutely. Our no-code decision engine lets you configure approval workflows, scoring rules, and document requirements to match your exact lending policies.

What's included in support?

All plans include email and chat support, dedicated account management, training materials, and quarterly business reviews. Enterprise customers receive priority support with 2-hour response times.
