Our Story

From frustration to innovation — how we're transforming lending

2023 - The Problem

Our founders spent years in private lending and saw the same problem everywhere: talented underwriters buried in spreadsheets, lenders losing deals to faster competitors, and borrowers frustrated by slow approvals.

2024 - The Solution

We built Mentyx.ai to solve this. Not another generic LOS, but a platform designed specifically for asset-based lenders who need speed without sacrificing accuracy.

2025 - The Future

Today, we're powering 50+ lenders across North America and Europe. Our AI processes millions in loans every month, and we're just getting started.
